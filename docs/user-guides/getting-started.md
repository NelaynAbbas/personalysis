## Account Setup

### First-time Login

1. You should have received an invitation email from your organization's administrator.
2. Click the "Accept Invitation" link in the email.
3. Create a secure password and complete your profile information.
4. Once complete, you'll be directed to the main dashboard.

### Profile Completion

For the best experience, we recommend completing your profile:

1. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu.
3. Fill in your professional details and preferences.
4. Save your changes.

## Dashboard Overview

The PersonalysisPro dashboard provides a central hub for all your activities:

- **Analytics Overview**: Key metrics and insights at a glance
- **Recent Surveys**: Quick access to your most recent survey projects
- **Client Management**: Access to your client profiles and data
- **Team Collaboration**: View and manage team activities
- **Resource Center**: Access to templates, guides, and training materials

## Creating Your First Survey

1. From the dashboard, click the "+ New Survey" button.
2. Select a template or start from scratch.
3. Configure your survey settings:
   - Title and description
   - Target audience
   - Survey duration
   - Response format
4. Add questions using our intuitive builder.
5. Preview your survey and make any necessary adjustments.
6. When ready, click "Publish" to make your survey live.

## Analyzing Results

As responses come in, PersonalysisPro automatically analyzes the data:

1. Navigate to "Surveys" in the main menu.
2. Select your survey from the list.
3. Click on "Analytics" to view comprehensive results.
4. Explore various visualization options:
   - Personality trait distributions
   - Demographic breakdowns
   - Comparison charts
   - Trend analysis

## Sharing Insights

Share valuable insights with your team or clients:

1. From the analytics view, click "Share" in the top-right corner.
2. Choose your sharing options:
   - Generate a shareable link
   - Export to PDF/PowerPoint
   - Schedule automated reports
   - Send direct email invitations
